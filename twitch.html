Twitch.tv Ethnographic Study
  • About

    The purpose of this study was to explore how people build online communities. Our team had an interest in the online world because more and more people are using it to connect with others. Little research has been done on how people build communities on live streaming websites, more specifically, communities on Twitch.

  • The Goal

    Our goal was to understand how streamers build communities on Twitch.

  • Steps to Achieve Goal

    1. Conduct observations on Twitch by watching streamers with different sized followings
    2. Create an interview protocol for streamers and viewers to streamline the interview process
    3. Reach out to streamers and viewers for interviews
    4. Conduct interviews and type up transcripts for analysis
    5. Analyze data collected from observations and interviews to formulate themes
    6. Identify themes, problems, solutions, and implications
    7. Present findings and final paper
      Project Summary

  • Skills

    Ethnography, interviews, research, analysis, writing

  • Lessons Learned

    I learned that ethnographic studies can provide insight into the social and organizational aspects and implications of information technology and digital media. I learned how to formulate interview questions that will lead to insightful answers and how to analyze data to find major themes.
